# The Knowledge Fortress: Alchemical Manual for Time Engineering

This manual defines the high-standard frameworks enforced within the CyberXiuXian Artisan Workshop. These are not suggestions; they are the physical and cognitive laws of the domain.

## 1. Buffer Engineering (The Law of Alchemical Slack)

- **Ethos**: Humans are biologically incapable of perfect prediction. Over-scheduling is a form of structural instability.
- **The Golden Ratio**: `Buffer = Total_Time * 0.20`.
- **Application**: For any 12-hour window, only 9.6 hours can be allocated to active tasks. The remaining 2.4 hours must be reserved for "Deep Work Overrun" or "Alchemical Friction."
- **Standard**: Any plan utilizing more than 80% of the physical window is a "Critical Integrity Failure."

## 2. Energy Mapping & Cognitive Saliency

- **The Peak-Trough-Rebound Cycle**:
  - **Peak (The Morning Forge)**: 09:00 - 12:00. Highest neuro-plasticity. Reserved for "Deep Refactors" and "Security Audits."
  - **Trough (The OPS Desert)**: 13:00 - 15:00. Low dopamine availability. Reserved for administrative cleanup and physical purging.
  - **Rebound (The Synthesis Sunset)**: 16:00 - 18:00. High integrative capacity. Reserved for "Phase Vision" and "Adversarial Reflection."

## 3. The 5-Whys Cognitive Audit (Root Cause Analysis)

- **Objective**: To strip away the "Ego" from the "Mission."
- **Process**: When a task is proposed, ask "Why" 5 times.
  - _Example_: Why fix this leak? -> To stabilize the graph. -> Why? -> To support 10k nodes. -> Why? -> To achieve Phase 12.
- **Success Criteria**: A task is only "Grounded" if its 5th 'Why' links directly to a Milestone in the Workshop Roadmap.

## 4. WIP Limits (The Kanban Seal)

- **Constraint**: No more than 3 active "Cognitive Threads" per session.
- **Penalty**: Exceeding WIP limits triggers "Context Switching Fatigue," reducing performance by 40% per additional task.
